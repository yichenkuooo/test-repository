"Like Air"

Like air we float kite words fragile signals and hearts flap purple wings Like air my struggling lungs seize YOU gasp relief release long-held breaths desire expanding capacity unknown welcoming gulps of future Like air I need clarity shot through with sunlight invisibly to buoy me towards an idea called us My dreams are mostly silent Does something besides distance obscure us? Like air intimacy should be a right for now it is only a promise Like air YOU travel my blood's hungry stream to every limb this is how for now we touch

Laura Hershey, "Like Air" from Laura Hershey: On the Life & Work of an American Master(2019)
